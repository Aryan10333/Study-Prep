### Optimized DSA Preparation Calendar (AI & GenAI Focused)

This is a modified 12-week daily calendar optimized specifically for **AI Engineering, GenAI Engineering, and Applied ML** interview loops. 

#### Core Adjustments:
1. **Bit Manipulation Removed**: Replaced with **String Parsing, Tokenization, and Trie structures** (critical for understanding LLM prompting, regex safety, and token matching).
2. **Hard DP Redundancies Pruned**: Replaced complex game-theory DP (like Burst Balloons) with high-frequency **Heap/Priority Queue and Graph Shortest Path algorithms** (critical for top-K vector search retrieval and multi-agent DAG dependencies).
3. **Structured Timed Mock Focus**: Keeping the 15-mock interview build-up in Weeks 10-11.
